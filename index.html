I AM: A WOMAN IN TECH

As a student at Lambda School, I am training to become a Full Stacks Web Developer. I aspire to create beautiful content and increase the diversity and minority representation in the field of technology. Being a millenial, it's important to me to not only be a consumer of this age of technology, but also a creator and producer.

I came from a health sciences background: biology, epidemiology, and biostatistics. During my graduate studies, the desire to bridge the world of health and tech became undeniable. More specifically, I hope to increase the dissemination of and accessibility to health information related to underserved or marginalized communities.
